[SET 1]
Section A Questions:
  1. What is the role of DNA in genetics?
  2. What is the primary source of energy for most ecosystems on Earth?
  3. What ancient civilization is known for its contributions to mathematics, including the development of the Pythagorean theorem?
  4. Which literary movement is associated with authors like F. Scott Fitzgerald, Ernest Hemingway, and Gertrude Stein?
  5. Which classic novel is set during the French Revolution and follows the experiences of the orphaned protagonist, Charles Darnay?

Section B Essay Questions:
  1. What is ecological succession, and how do pioneer species contribute to both primary and secondary succession?
  2. Explain the significance of genetic mutations in evolution, highlighting their role in genetic diversity and adaptation.
  3. Discuss the significance of the Nile River in the development of ancient Egyptian civilization. Highlight the role of agriculture, the annual flooding, and the cultural and religious aspects tied to the Nile.
