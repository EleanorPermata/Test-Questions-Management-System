[SET 2]
Section A Questions:
  1. In which ancient civilization did Hammurabi's Code, one of the earliest known written legal codes, originate?
  2. What ancient civilization is known for its contributions to mathematics, including the development of the Pythagorean theorem?
  3. Who is often regarded as the "father of the Renaissance" for his contributions to art, science, and anatomy?
  4. What is the primary source of energy for most ecosystems on Earth?
  5. Where does the process of translation occur in a eukaryotic cell?

Section B Essay Questions:
  1. Discuss the significance of cell division in the growth, development, and maintenance of living organisms. Explore the key phases of the cell cycle and highlight the differences between mitosis and meiosis. Additionally, explain how errors in cell division can lead to diseases such as cancer.
  2. What is ecological succession, and how do pioneer species contribute to both primary and secondary succession?
  3. Examine the impact of the printing press on the spread of Renaissance ideas. Discuss how the invention of the printing press by Johannes Gutenberg facilitated the dissemination of knowledge, transformed communication, and contributed to the cultural and intellectual flourishing of the Renaissance.
